About Me

I am 20 years old, Brazilian, and I am passionate about a lot of things: books, dogs, photography, writing, travelling and among many other passions there also is languages.

I speak Portuguese, which is my mother language, English, Spanish, and I am currently learning French (by myself, I must add).

I think that when you learn a language you are not simply learning it, you are learning a new culture and can travel to the countries that speak this language and interact with the people from there. Also, I believe that there is no better way to find job opportunities and make friends than speaking more than one idioms.

How it started...

This website was born from the wish of helping others to learn different idioms.

It can be very trick at times during the process of learning, it being on the writing or on the communications, and I know very well the feeling, I have learned fluently three languages. Sadly, I can't help a lot on, for example, the communication, but I do wish to make the learning process easier with the tips I have on this website. But don't give up, being able to speak another language fluently is priceless.

If you want to get to know me more or follow my daily tips about languages, you can find me on Instagram, this is the platform I use the most, but you can also find me on Twitter and Linkedin.
